who am i?


I am Jappreet Singh, and I am passionate about designing unconventional yet functional and visually pleasing interfaces for both mobile and web platforms. My goal is to create unique user experiences that captivate and engage users.


As a designer, I have a deep appreciation for the power of design elements such as typography, colors, fonts, compositions, illustrations, and motions. I find joy in experimenting with different combinations of these elements to create visually stunning and impactful designs. By carefully selecting and harmonizing these elements, I strive to create interfaces that not only meet functional requirements but also evoke emotional responses from users.




who do i do?


Currently, I am pursuing a B Tech. in Computer Science Engineering from Chandigarh University in Punjab. This academic journey has equipped me with a strong foundation in the technical aspects of interface design. I am constantly learning and honing my skills to stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in the field. I believe that a solid understanding of the underlying technologies is essential for creating innovative and effective interfaces.


My passion for design extends beyond the confines of my education. I am addicted to exploring new ideas, pushing boundaries, and seeking inspiration from various sources. Whether it's studying the latest design trends, analyzing successful design case studies, or immersing myself in the works of renowned designers, I constantly strive to expand my creative horizons.


I take great pride in my ability to combine aesthetics with functionality. I understand that a visually pleasing interface is only effective if it is intuitive and user-friendly. Therefore, I place a strong emphasis on usability and user-centered design principles. By conducting thorough research and incorporating user feedback throughout the design process, I ensure that my interfaces not only look great but also provide a seamless and enjoyable user experience.




in a crux


Looking forward, I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead in my design career. I am eager to collaborate with talented individuals and contribute my skills to projects that have a positive impact on users. I believe that through continuous learning, dedication, and a passion for design, I can make a meaningful difference in the world of interface design.
